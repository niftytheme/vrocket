Let's Encrypt is a free Secure Socket Layer certificate that keeps your website traffic safe by encrypting the data that is transferred between our servers and their computer. Let's Encrypt allows you to enable your website to run in the secure HTTPS setting, as oppose to the open HTTP setting.
