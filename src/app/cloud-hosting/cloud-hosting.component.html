Let's Encrypt is a free Secure Socket Layer certificate that keeps your website traffic safe by encrypting the data that is transferred between our servers and their computer. Let's Encrypt allows you to enable your website to run in the secure HTTPS setting, as oppose to the open HTTP setting. VRocket provides a free Let's Encrypt SSL for all website hosting plans and we enable it automatically. Our Let's Encrypt SSL certificate will secure any of your domains including subdomains at no additional cost.
